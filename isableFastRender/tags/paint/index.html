Painting & Styling Backdrop

Materials Needed: Backdrop Material: A large sheet of foam board, Masonite, or another rigid material that fits the size of your module. Alternatively, you can paint directly on the module backdrop if it’s made of a suitable material. Paints: Acrylic paints are recommended for their versatility and quick drying time. Colors for the sky, mountains, hills, and any other elements you want to include.
